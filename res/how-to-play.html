How to play citySquare

Aim

The aim of city square is to match up the tiles from the tray on the right on the grid as best as is possible in a given amount of time

Points are awarded for COMPLEATED cities (the orange bits i know i need better graphics) and roads. They are however taken away if the city or road is UNCOMPLEATED

It is also important to note that a one big city will score substantially more than two smaller cities. i.e. a four tile city is worth more than double two, two tile cities

Also no tile must face off the grid (only grass can touch the edges) and any invalid tile is invisible to the scoring mechanism

Controls

The controls are very simple, left-click a tile to pick it up and left-click again either in the grid or tray to place the tile

Tiles can be rotated by right-clicking them or by using the scroll wheel while you are holding a tile

All of the tiles currently on the grid can e shifted around using the arrow keys

To view a breakup of your score so far you can press hold tab
